- **Герои и злодеи** — любимые [персонажи](articles/dream_team/screenwriter.md);

- **Игровая [культура](../../2.1_society/cause_and_effect_relationships/articles/why_rules_work.md)** — [косплей](../../2.1_society/how_and_where_find_friends/articles/fandom.md), [киберспорт](articles/game_culture/esports.md), мемы;
